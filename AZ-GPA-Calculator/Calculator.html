Everything You Need

Designed to support students across different universities and grading systems.

📊
GPA Calculator
Add courses with credit hours and grades. Semester GPA is computed instantly using your chosen university's grading scale.
🗂️
Semester History
Save completed semesters locally. Browse your full academic record and cumulative GPA at a glance — no internet required.
🏫
Multi-University Scales
Switch between the Generic 4.0 Scale, Generic 5.0 Scale, and custom university grading systems with a single tap.
🔮
GPA Forecasting (Phase 2)
Simulate target grades to plan your path to a desired cumulative GPA before the semester even begins.
🧪
Scenario Simulator (Phase 2)
Run "what-if" analyses. Explore how dropping or retaking a course changes your overall GPA trajectory.
🤖
AI Advisory (Phase 3)
Receive intelligent recommendations based on your academic trends to help you achieve your study goals.

Roadmap

Three phases to a complete academic intelligence platform.

Phase 1 – MVP ✅ Current
  • Add courses with grades & credit hours
  • Semester GPA calculator
  • Cumulative GPA tracker
  • University grading scale selector
  • Save & persist semester records
  • Edit / delete saved records
Phase 2 – Analytics 🔄 Upcoming
  • GPA forecasting engine
  • Scenario ("what-if") simulator
  • Custom grading scale builder
  • Export semester report (PDF / CSV)
Phase 3 – Intelligence 🚀 Future
  • AI-powered academic advisory
  • Cloud sync & device backup
  • Cross-university GPA analytics
  • Admin dashboard for institutions
