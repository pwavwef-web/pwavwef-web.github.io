What is an ATS?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by most companies to automatically scan and filter job applications before a recruiter ever sees them. It looks for specific keywords, formatting, and structure.

Studies show that over 75% of CVs are rejected by ATS before reaching a human. Our ATS Score Checker analyses your CV the same way these systems do — so you can fix issues before they cost you an interview.
