About This Project

What Is the AZ GPA Calculator?

AZ Learner — the squad companion app at UCC — is growing. The AZ GPA Calculator is a dedicated standalone tool we're building to expand our reach across Ghanaian universities. It's built for every student, regardless of their school's grading system.

🏗️

Why a standalone GPA Calculator?

AZ Learner is currently focused on UCC students — print & deliver, course circles, past questions, and campus tools. As the main app matures at UCC, the AZ GPA Calculator is our vehicle to scale into universities like KNUST, UG, UDS, and beyond. It's designed to work for any grading system, any university.

📊

GPA Forecasting

Predict your end-of-semester GPA in real time as you enter your grades — no more surprises when results drop.

🎯

Score Calculator

📚

Grade History

Why We're Doing This

Students shouldn't be in the dark about their own grades

Most university students only find out their GPA when official results are released — often weeks after exams. By then, it's too late to course-correct.

Others rely on mental math or scattered spreadsheets that are error-prone and don't give a complete picture. The frustration of being "surprised" by a lower GPA is real — and preventable.

AZ Learner solved this for UCC. Now we're building a dedicated tool to bring the same power to every Ghanaian university. But first, we need to deeply understand your experience.
