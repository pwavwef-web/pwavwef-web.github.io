Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Welcome to AZ Learner ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from you when you use our platform at azlearner.me, our web app at app.azlearner.me, and the AZ Learner Android application (collectively, "the Service"), how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, school/institution, and password (stored in hashed form).
  • Profile Data: Optional profile details such as your program, level, and profile photo.
  • User-Generated Content: Blog posts, chat messages, event submissions, past questions, and any other content you contribute to the platform.
  • Print Requests: Documents you upload when using the Print Service. These are handled securely and deleted after printing.
  • Feedback & Communications: Any messages you send to us via email, WhatsApp, or the in-app feedback system.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log Data: IP address, date and time of access, and error logs to help us diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Location Data: If you use the Campus Map feature, we may request your approximate location to help you navigate campus. This is only used while the feature is active and is not stored on our servers.

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Processing: Payments made for premium features or print services are processed through Paystack. We do not store your card details. Please review Paystack's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Analytics: We use Google AdSense and similar tools to understand usage patterns. These services may place cookies on your device. See the Cookies section below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process print requests and payments
  • Personalise your experience on the platform
  • Send you important updates, announcements, and notifications related to your account or the Service
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Monitor and analyse usage trends to improve features
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers who help us operate the Service (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing). They are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose your information to comply with legal processes.
  • Safety: If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of AZ Learner, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymise your data within 7 business days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Uploaded print documents are automatically deleted from our servers within 48 hours of your print request being fulfilled or cancelled.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Service. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as login sessions.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Service (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Service. To opt out of Google's personalised advertising, visit adssettings.google.com.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
  • Hashed and salted passwords ‚Äî we never store plain-text passwords
  • Access controls limiting which team members can access user data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Children's Privacy

AZ Learner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@azlearner.me and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at info@azlearner.me. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., social media platforms, Google Play Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via the app or by email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
