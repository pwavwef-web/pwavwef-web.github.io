Marketing & Outreach Strategies

18–24px · section titles and card headers

BODY TEXT — Regular (400)

AZ Learner is the all-in-one student companion for university students in Ghana. Built with students, for students.

14–16px · Minimum 1.5 line-height for readability

Typography Rules

  • • Never use more than 2 font sizes on a single graphic
  • • Minimum body font size: 14px (10.5pt in print materials)
  • • Never use light grey text on white backgrounds — use on dark only

Logo Usage Rules

Correct Usage

  • • Full-colour logo on dark backgrounds
  • • All-white logo on coloured backgrounds
  • • Minimum clear space equal to height of the letter "A" on all sides
  • • Minimum logo width: 80px digital / 25mm print

Never Do This

  • • Do not alter, stretch or rotate the logo
  • • Do not place the logo on busy or clashing backgrounds
  • • Do not add shadows, outlines or effects to the logo
  • • Do not use old or unofficial versions of the logo

Content Creation Guidelines

Always include the AZ Learner logo on every co-branded graphic or flyer you produce

Use real, student-friendly language — avoid corporate-speak and buzzwords

Include a clear call-to-action and your referral link on every promotional post

Add alt-text to all images on social media for accessibility

Do not create materials that could be seen as misleading or exaggerated claims about the platform
