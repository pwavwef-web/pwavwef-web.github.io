Welcome

Congratulations on becoming an AZ Learner Campus Ambassador! You are now part of an elite squad of student leaders who are driving academic success across universities in Ghana. This handbook is your go-to reference for everything you need to know about your role, your responsibilities, and how to make the most of this opportunity.

"You are not just an ambassador for a platform — you are a champion for better student outcomes on your campus."

Your Role

As an AZ Learner Ambassador, you are the bridge between the platform and your campus community. Your primary responsibilities include:

  • 1

    Promote AZ Learner on Campus

    Use social media, flyers, word-of-mouth and campus events to raise awareness of the platform.

  • 2

    Host Study Groups & Events

    Organise regular study circles and campus events that showcase AZ Learner's features.

  • 3

    Recruit New Students

    Drive sign-ups using your personal referral link, targeting a minimum of 10 new students per month.

  • 4

    Attend Monthly Check-ins

    Join the monthly ambassador sessions (virtual or in-person) to share updates and receive new materials.

  • 5

    Submit Monthly Reports

    Log your activities in the ambassador portal by the last day of each month to claim your achievement points.

Your Benefits

Achievement Points

Earn points for every activity — redeemable for rewards and recognition on the leaderboard.

Official Certificate

Receive a recognised certificate of completion to strengthen your CV and portfolio.

Priority Feature Access

Be the first to test and use new AZ Learner features before they're released to the public.

Recommendation Letter

Outstanding ambassadors receive a personal letter of recommendation from the AZ Learner leadership team.

Code of Conduct

As a representative of AZ Learner, you are expected to uphold the following standards at all times:

Be professional — Represent the platform with integrity in all interactions, online and offline.

Be accurate — Only share information confirmed in official AZ Learner materials. Never make unsanctioned promises.

Respect privacy — Do not share fellow students' personal data in public spaces or without consent.

Do not use the ambassador role to promote other commercial products or personal businesses without written approval.
