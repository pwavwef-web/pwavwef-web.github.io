Pre-Event Checklist

Start planning at least 2 weeks before your event. Tap each item to mark it done.

Define event objectives and desired outcomes
Book venue or set up virtual meeting link (Google Meet / Zoom)
Create promotional flyer using AZ Learner brand colours
Post on social media 3 weeks, 1 week and 1 day before the event
Send personal WhatsApp invites to at least 10 key students
Prepare slides, handouts or shared AZ Learner session
Test all technology (projector, screen share, audio, internet)

Run of Show (90-minute Template)

0:00

Welcome & Introductions

Greet attendees, introduce yourself as the AZ Learner ambassador, state the session goal.

0:05

Energiser / Icebreaker

Quick activity to warm up the group — see the icebreaker ideas below.

0:15

Main Content / Workshop

Cover the core material — can include a walkthrough, presentation or group exercise.

1:00

Group Discussion / Breakout Activity

Small groups tackle a challenge or question related to the topic. Report back with key insights.

1:20

Q&A

Open floor for questions. Have your referral link ready for anyone who wants to sign up.

1:30

Close & Next Steps

Summarise key takeaways, share your referral link, announce the next session, collect sign-out.

Icebreaker Ideas

One-Word Check-In

Each person says one word about how they feel today. Fast, no pressure, warms everyone up instantly.

Teach It in 30 Seconds

Pick a concept from the topic and give 30 seconds to explain it. Great for quick knowledge checks.

Two Truths One Lie

Study-themed version: two facts and one myth about the topic. Group votes on the lie.
