Conversation Starters

Never lead with "download this app." Always start with a genuine question or observation.

Opener 1 – Exam Angle

"How are you preparing for the upcoming exams? Have you found good past questions yet?"

Opener 2 – Curiosity Hook

"Have you tried any study apps? I found one that's been really useful this semester."

Opener 3 – Direct Recommendation

"There's a free platform a lot of us are using called AZ Learner — want me to send you the link?"

Opener 4 – Problem-Solution

"I used to struggle keeping track of everything — then I found something that organises it all for me. Want to hear about it?"

Objection Handling

Every great recruiter prepares for objections. These are the most common ones you'll hear:

"I don't have time."

Response: "It actually saves you time — it organises your study schedule, so you stop wasting time figuring out what to do next. Takes 2 minutes to sign up."

"Is it free?"

Response: "Completely free to sign up. No credit card, no subscription, no catch. It's built for students."

"I already have my own study system."

Response: "It works alongside whatever you're doing — the past questions feature alone is worth signing up for. It literally saves hours of searching online."

"I'll check it out later."

Response: "Here, let me send you the link right now — takes 2 seconds and then you have it when you need it." [Send link immediately]

"I don't use study apps."

Response: "Totally fine — AZ Learner also has a print & deliver service and campus tools that most students end up using way before the study features."

Top Ambassador Tips

1.

Send 10 personal messages before posting in a group. Personalised outreach converts 3× better.

2.

Always share your own story — "This feature helped me with my finals" is more convincing than any sales pitch.

3.

Follow up with anyone who expressed interest but didn't sign up. One follow-up message doubles your conversion rate.

4.

Check your portal dashboard weekly to track your referral count. Set a personal monthly target and track progress.
