General

What is AZ Learner?
AZ Learner is the all-in-one student companion built for university students ‚Äî particularly at UCC, Cape Coast. It combines tools like a GPA calculator, event tracker, study countdown timers, a student blog, past questions, campus maps, and a community chat, all in one sleek platform.
Who can use AZ Learner?
AZ Learner is designed for any student who wants to stay organized, connected, and ahead of their academic journey. While it has special features tailored for University of Cape Coast (UCC) students, students from any institution can benefit from the productivity and social tools available.
Is AZ Learner free to use?
Yes! AZ Learner is free to use for all students. Most core features are completely free. Some premium features may be available through optional upgrade plans, which offer extra benefits like enhanced printing credits and VIP status on the leaderboard.
How do I get started?
Simply visit app.azlearner.me in your browser, or download the Android app from the Google Play Store. Create an account with your name, school, and a password, and you're ready to go!

How does the Print Service work?
The AZ Learner Print Service lets you upload documents (PDF, Word, images) directly in the app and have them printed at a designated point on campus. Simply upload your file, choose your print settings, pay a small fee, and pick up your documents. It's fast, convenient, and saves you the stress of long queues.
How does the GPA Calculator work?
Enter your course name, credit hours, and the grade you received (or expect to receive) for each course. The calculator uses standard grade-point weighting to give you your semester GPA and cumulative GPA. You can save results and track your academic progress over time.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes! The AZ Learner Android app is available on the Google Play Store. An iOS version is planned for the future. You can also use the full web app at app.azlearner.me on any device.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link in your inbox. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
Is my data safe on AZ Learner?
Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely and is never sold to third parties. For full details on what we collect and how we use it, please read our Privacy Policy.
Can I delete my account?
Yes. You can request account deletion by emailing us at info@azlearner.me with the subject "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 7 business days and permanently remove your data from our systems.

Ambassador Program

What is the AZ Learner Ambassador Program?
The Ambassador Program allows passionate students to represent AZ Learner on their campuses. Ambassadors help spread the word, onboard new users, and organize campus events. In return, they receive exclusive perks, recognition, and the chance to grow their leadership skills.
How do I become an ambassador?
You can apply to become an AZ Learner Ambassador by filling out the sign-up form on the Ambassador Sign-Up page. Applications are reviewed periodically, and successful applicants will be contacted via email.
