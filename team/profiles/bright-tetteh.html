About

As a finance student at the University of Cape Coast, I find myself deeply involved in an exciting and rewarding project. Specifically, I am providing support to AZ learners, assisting them in both the development and subsequent deployment of the AZ podcast. This role within the team is one that I genuinely cherish and enjoy. Contributing to the creation and launch of the podcast has been a fantastic learning experience for me, complementing my studies in finance. I find great satisfaction in helping others develop their skills and bring their creative projects to life. What makes my involvement even more enjoyable is the collaborative and supportive atmosphere within the team. It's a privilege to work alongside such talented individuals who are equally passionate about the AZ podcast. Beyond my academic pursuits and my involvement with the podcast, there is also a rather peculiar, yet amusing, fact about me. A fun fact, if you will. This quirky tidbit is that I possess the rather unique ability to fit five pieces of yam, each measuring 5cm cubed, into my mouth simultaneously. It's a talent that often surprises people, and while it may not be particularly useful in the world of finance, it certainly makes for an interesting conversation starter.
