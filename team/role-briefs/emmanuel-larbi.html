Role Brief

Emmanuel Larbi

Administrative Assistant — AZ Learner

Role Mission

Keep AZ Learner's daily operations running smoothly by managing user accounts, curating platform content and creating an engaging, welcoming experience for every student on the platform.

What This Role Owns

User account management: creation, updates and issue resolution
Content curation: reviewing, organising and publishing platform resources
User support: responding to queries, complaints and feedback
Engagement initiatives: activities that encourage students to actively use AZ Learner
Record keeping: maintaining accurate logs of platform activity and user data
Coordination with CEO and other team members on operational tasks

Weekly KPIs

User Queries Resolved

All user support requests responded to within 24 hours; resolved within 72 hours

Content Items Reviewed

Number of resources reviewed for accuracy and relevance this week

Engagement Initiative Completed

At least one student engagement activity planned or executed per week

Account Issues Resolved

Zero outstanding user account issues older than 5 business days

Weekly Operations Report

Brief summary of key operational activities submitted to CEO every Friday

Success Looks Like

Students receive fast, helpful responses to their queries and feel supported
Platform content is accurate, organised and easy for students to navigate
User engagement metrics (return visits, feature usage) trend upward month-on-month
The platform runs smoothly with no administrative bottlenecks or unresolved issues

Accountability

Monthly 1-on-1 with CEO reviewing operational performance and user satisfaction
Weekly ops report ensures transparent visibility of activity and any blockers
User satisfaction feedback reviewed with CEO quarterly

Growth & Development

Customer support best practices and CRM tools
Content management and information architecture for digital platforms
Community management and student engagement strategies
Project management and operational workflow design
