Role Brief

Bright Tetteh

Lead Strategic Designer — AZ Learner

Role Mission

Shape and own AZ Learner's visual identity across every touchpoint — from the platform UI to marketing materials and podcast artwork. Ensure every design decision is strategic, consistent and communicates the brand's mission with clarity.

What This Role Owns

AZ Learner brand visual guidelines (colours, typography, iconography)
UI/UX design for platform pages and new features
Marketing and social media graphic templates
Podcast cover art, episode artwork and visual identity
Presentation decks, pitch materials and event collateral
Design review and quality control on all published visual content

Weekly KPIs

Design Deliverables Completed

Number of requested design assets delivered on time this week

Brand Consistency Checks

All published content reviewed against brand guidelines before release

UI Improvement Suggestion

At least one UI/UX improvement proposal submitted to the Developer per sprint

Podcast Visual Assets Ready

Episode artwork and supporting graphics ready at least 48 hours before publish

Success Looks Like

AZ Learner's brand is instantly recognisable and consistently applied across all channels
All design requests are completed on schedule with no last-minute delays
Platform UI receives positive feedback from users for being clean and intuitive
Podcast visuals enhance the show's professional identity and social shareability

Accountability

Monthly 1-on-1 with the CEO to review design output and brand quality
Design portfolio updated each quarter to reflect completed work
Collaboration review with Marketing and Developer to ensure design-to-production alignment

Growth & Development

Advanced UI/UX tools (Figma, Adobe XD) and design systems
Motion graphics and interactive design for social media
Design thinking and user research methodologies
Brand strategy and visual storytelling at scale
