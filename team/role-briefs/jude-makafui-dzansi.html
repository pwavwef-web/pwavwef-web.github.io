Role Brief

Jude Makafui Dzansi

Head of Communications & Marketing — AZ Learner

Role Mission

Drive AZ Learner's marketing strategy and public communications to grow brand awareness, attract new users and retain the existing community — ensuring every message is clear, consistent and aligned with the platform's mission.

What This Role Owns

Content calendar: weekly social media posts, announcements and campaigns
All AZ Learner social media accounts (posting, monitoring, engagement)
External communications: announcements, press, campus partnerships
Email marketing campaigns and newsletters
Marketing analytics: tracking reach, engagement and conversion
Coordination with Design for visual assets and with Ambassadors for campus campaigns

Weekly KPIs

Social Media Posts Published

Minimum of 3 posts per week across active platforms

Follower / Reach Growth

Track week-on-week growth in social media followers and post reach

Engagement Rate

Likes, shares, comments and saves as a percentage of reach

Marketing-Driven Sign-ups

New user registrations linked to a marketing campaign or social media post

Content Calendar Updated

Next two weeks of content planned and shared with the team by every Monday

Success Looks Like

AZ Learner's social presence is consistent, growing and driving new sign-ups
Every campaign is planned in advance and executed without last-minute scrambles
Marketing communications are clear, compelling and free of errors
Monthly analytics report presented to the CEO with actionable insights

Accountability

Monthly 1-on-1 with CEO reviewing marketing performance and upcoming strategy
Monthly analytics summary (reach, engagement, conversions) shared with team
Content calendar reviewed weekly — no post goes live without prior approval for major campaigns

Growth & Development

Digital marketing strategy and paid social advertising
Content writing, storytelling and brand copywriting
SEO, email marketing and analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mailchimp)
Crisis communications and public relations best practices
