Role Brief

Francis Pwavwe

Founder & CEO — AZ Learner

Role Mission

Lead AZ Learner's vision, strategy and team. As CEO, Francis is the final decision-maker, the culture setter, and the primary driver of the platform's growth and impact for Ghanaian university students.

What This Role Owns

Product vision and long-term roadmap for AZ Learner
Hiring, onboarding and developing the admin team
Final approval on major platform features, pricing and policies
Strategic partnerships with universities, sponsors and vendors
Financial oversight and fundraising strategy
Brand identity, values and culture definition
External representation: media, events and public communications

Weekly KPIs

Platform Active Users

Track weekly active user growth vs prior week

Team Weekly Check-in Completion

Run 15-min team check-in: What's done, what's blocked, what's next

3 CEO-Only Strategic Tasks Completed

Identify and complete the 3 tasks only the CEO can do this week

Revenue & Financial Health Review

Review weekly revenue, outstanding payments and budget status with Finance

1 Partnership or Growth Conversation

Initiate or advance at least one external partnership, sponsor or collaboration

Success Looks Like

The team knows exactly what they're working on and why, with no confusion about priorities
AZ Learner grows its user base month-on-month across Ghanaian campuses
Revenue covers operating costs and enables further product investment
The culture is one of accountability, growth and student-first thinking
Partnerships are formalised and delivering measurable value

Accountability

As CEO, Francis holds all other team members accountable through monthly 1-on-1 reviews. The CEO's own accountability is self-directed and mission-driven:

Monthly self-review against platform growth targets
Quarterly reflection on team culture and morale
Open-door policy — team can raise concerns directly

Growth & Development

Executive leadership and stakeholder management skills
Ed-tech product strategy and market analysis
Fundraising, grant writing and investor relations
Public speaking and brand representation
