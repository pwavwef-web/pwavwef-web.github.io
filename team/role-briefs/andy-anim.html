Role Brief

Andy Anim

Chief of Ambassadors — AZ Learner

Role Mission

Build, lead and sustain a high-energy network of campus ambassadors who actively promote AZ Learner's brand, drive new student sign-ups and create a visible, trusted presence across Ghanaian university campuses.

What This Role Owns

Ambassador recruitment, screening and onboarding process
Ambassador training, motivation and performance management
Campus outreach campaigns, events and activations
Ambassador communication channels and reporting
Tracking ambassador-driven sign-ups and campus reach
Coordinating with Marketing to align messaging and campaigns

Weekly KPIs

Active Ambassador Count

Track number of ambassadors who completed at least one action this week

Ambassador-Driven New Sign-ups

Number of new AZ Learner registrations attributed to ambassador referrals

Campus Events / Activations

Number of campus outreach activities completed or planned this week

Ambassador Engagement Rate

Percentage of ambassadors who responded to weekly update / check-in

New Ambassador Onboarded

At least one new ambassador recruited and onboarded per month

Success Looks Like

A growing, motivated ambassador network active on multiple campuses
Ambassador referrals drive a measurable share of new monthly sign-ups
Ambassadors understand AZ Learner's value proposition and communicate it clearly
Low ambassador churn — they stay motivated and engaged over time

Accountability

Monthly 1-on-1 with the CEO to review ambassador performance and campus reach
Weekly ambassador activity summary shared with the CEO
Responsible for applying the 3-Strike system fairly within the ambassador programme

Growth & Development

People management and remote team leadership skills
Brand ambassador programme design and best practices
Data tracking and reporting on outreach performance
Public speaking, persuasion and recruitment interviewing
