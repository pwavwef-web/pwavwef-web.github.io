Meeting Wrap-Up Checklist

After completing all individual evaluations, close the meeting by confirming:
✅ Each member has received at least one piece of specific, actionable feedback.
✅ Each member has committed to at least one measurable improvement by the next meeting.
✅ All department heads have updated their action items in the shared task tracker.
✅ The next meeting date has been confirmed and communicated to all members.
✅ Any cross-functional blockers identified during evaluations have been assigned an owner and a resolution deadline.
