Team Evaluation Guide

Detailed, role-specific evaluation criteria for each team member. Use this guide during the meeting to assess performance, identify strengths, and agree on next steps.

📅  Meeting Evaluation — AZ Learner Core Team

📌 How to Use This Guide

  1. Expand each team member's card using the toggle arrow or the Expand All button.
  2. Work through every evaluation criterion and rate it 1–5 using the rating boxes (click to fill).
  3. Ask the listed Guided Questions to prompt discussion and gather specific evidence.
  4. Record your observations in the Live Notes text area for each member.
  5. Review Focus Areas (red = needs improvement, green = doing well) before wrapping up each member's segment.
  6. Use the Print button to print or save a PDF of the completed evaluations.

Rating Scale

1 – Poor / Not evident
2 – Below expectations
3 – Meets expectations
4 – Exceeds expectations
5 – Outstanding

Francis Pwavwe

Founder & CEO
▾
📊 Evaluation Criteria
Criterion What to Look For Weight Rating (1–5)
Vision Clarity Can clearly articulate where AZ Learner is heading in the next 3–6 months, with measurable milestones. 25%
Decision-Making Quality Recent product or operational decisions are well-reasoned, documented, and communicated to the team in a timely manner. 20%
Team Leadership & Morale Team members feel motivated, heard, and supported. Conflicts are resolved constructively. One-on-ones or check-ins happen regularly. 20%
Product Ownership Actively tracks feature progress, user feedback, and platform bugs. Can list the top 3 product priorities right now. 20%
Stakeholder Communication External partners, collaborators, and university contacts are kept informed. Responds promptly and professionally. 15%
💬 Guided Questions to Ask
  • What are the three most important milestones AZ Learner must hit in the next 60 days, and what is the current status of each?
  • Describe a difficult decision you made recently. What data or input informed it, and would you make the same call again?
  • How do you currently measure whether the team is motivated and aligned? What feedback have you received?
  • Which feature on the platform has gotten the best user response recently, and which one is underperforming? What do you plan to do about the underperforming one?
  • How many active users does AZ Learner have right now, and what is the trend over the last month?
  • If you had to name one area where you believe you could improve as a leader, what would it be and what's your plan to improve it?

Strategic Planning Product Roadmap Delegation to Team Leads Consistent Check-in Cadence External Partnerships Deadline Accountability

Financial Record Accuracy Income (CV sales, print services, subscriptions) and expenditure records are up-to-date, balanced, and can be presented in the meeting with specific figures. 25%
