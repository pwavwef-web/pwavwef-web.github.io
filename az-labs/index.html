🚀 Product Roadmap

AZ Learner Lab is building a career ecosystem — not just tools. Here's the plan.

🎯
✅ Phase 0 · Month 0–1

Foundation

Clarify positioning + clean infrastructure
✓Core identity defined: "Career acceleration and academic optimization platform for African university students"
✓AZ Career Engine chosen as Flagship MVP — solves urgent pain, students pay for career help, scales beyond University of Cape Coast (UCC)
🧱
🔨 Phase 1 · Month 1–3

MVP — AZ Career Engine

Small but powerful
✓CV Generator with ATS-optimized templates
✓Cover Letter Generator
✓WhatsApp automation: request intake, payment confirmation, file delivery
✓Basic dashboard (lightweight backend)
Pricing: CV Rewrite ₵25–₵40 CV + Cover Letter ₵60 Premium Optimization ₵80
📈
⚡ Phase 2 · Month 3–6

Authority + Growth

Build credibility + trust layer
→AZ Thought Lab Launch: Weekly 10–15 min episodes — interviews, internships, scholarships, academic hacks
→ATS Score Checker: Upload CV → formatting score + keyword match + improvement checklist
→Move from service business toward tech product
→Campus ambassador network + LinkedIn content engine
🌍
🌍 Phase 3 · Month 6–9

Expansion + Differentiation

Build the ladder you want to climb
→Abroad Readiness Hub: SOP writing assistant, scholarship directory, visa guides, alumni mentorship matching
→Digital Badge System: Shareable LinkedIn badges + verified "AZ Career Ready" certificates
→Gamification for CV workshops, interview training, mini courses
→Institutional partnerships + department collaborations
🧠
🧠 Phase 4 · Month 9–12

Product Intelligence

Ecosystem, not just a tool
→Student Data Dashboard: popular career paths, in-demand skills, interview trends, hiring patterns
→Insights → Industry Reports → Partnerships → Revenue
→Corporate recruitment partnerships — companies pay to access talent pool
→Subscription tier (₵20/month) for full tools access
Monetization Evolution: Pay-per-service → Subscription → Institutional → Corporate Recruitment
